Human Health business is conducted through Human Biologicals Institute (HBI), a division of Indian Immunologicals Ltd. It was formed in the year 1998, when the Govt. of India requested IIL to produce a modern tissue culture anti- rabies vaccine in order to phase out the unsafe Nerve Tissue Vaccine (NTV). The first manufacturing facility of HBI was set up at Ooty in 1998, one of the largest of its kind in India that has rolled out first Vero cell culture rabies vaccine in 2000. The manufacturing units conform to WHO-GMP, ISO 9001: 2015, EMS 14001:2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007 standards.

IIL set up its other manufacturing facilities at Hyderabad for DPT, DT, TT and Hepatitis-B vaccine. This Plant was inaugurated by the then Honorable President of India, Late Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam in 2003. Today, IIL is a leading player in human vaccine segment, both in single and multiple dosage forms.

Today Human Health division of IIL has emerged as a leading player in the vaccine segment in India and Nepal. In year 2014 IIL has forayed into clinically proven wellness products that will enrich the overall health. The marketing arm of Human Health reaches the entire geography of India and Nepal through valued products and services in line with the Vision & Mission of IIL.


Line of Business

Entry of Indian Immunologicals Limited into Anti Rabies Vaccine (ARV) helped state & central government Institution to phase out old reactogenic nerve tissue vaccine. IIL is one of the preferred and major suppliers to the state and central government Institutions. In addition IIL is also a major supplier to the universal immunization program (UIP) run by the Ministry of health and family welfare for measles, DPT, Hepatitis-B and TT vaccines. IIL is instrumental in implementing intradermal route of administration of Anti Rabies Vaccine (IDARV) in the country to minimize the cost of usage of ARV for the state government institutions.
